Projects

Investigating Motion in Functional Brain MRIs

Compared 2 methods for registering image volumes from functional MRI sequences: the traditional "register to the first image volume" method and a novel method using a directed acyclic graph based governed by the Markov property. Demonstrated that the novel method produced results 5x better than those of the traditional method.

Characterizing Images of Diseases with Multiple Subtypes

Collaborated with a junior faculty member to implement a process for identifying regions of thoracic CTs with similar COPD presentations across a large population of subjects. Used clustering, dimensionality reduction, and regression techniques to predict a clinical value associated with each scan and analyzed the model to justify its predictions.

Registration of Mammogram and Volumetric Breast Ultrasound Images

Proof of concept project showed that mammograms and volumetric breast ultrasound images can be registered to potentialy increase confidence in breast cancer diagnoses prior to a biopsy. Identified weak points in the registration pipeline and developed a plan to address them.
